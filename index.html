About Me

My name is Andrew Harper, I am a self taught Alla Prima oil artist, mainly landscapes and black canvas futuristic paintings on a number of different sized stretched canvas. Due to a spinal injury I am now retired and apart from my Army pension I rely on selling my work, or by offering my other services as a source of income (see below). All my work is unique in that no two paintings will ever be exactly the same unless purchased as a high quality giclee print. Each piece has a unique number, title, signed and dated and most are available for purchase via the gallery page, I sincerely hope you enjoy them and hopefully make a purchase!

What is Alla Prima

"Alla Prima" (latin) is an old style of painting in oils used by some of the old masters, basically it means wet-on-wet, where each layer is painted over the last allowing easier blending of colours, some of you may recognise this style as it was famously shown on television "The Joy of Painting" with the late Bob Ross who inspired me to take up painting in this style. Oil paintings can take several weeks or even months to completely dry depending on how much paint is applied, this allows for retouching and additional content to be added!
